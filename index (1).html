About Us

QUICKPLASMA is a website with the aim of providing a safe platform for blood donation management system as well as an option for instant blood delivery app to the Hospitals.

This initiative is taken by a team of 4 Third year AI&ML and CSE students.

This website is still under development.



Our Mission



  1. To develop a safe platform for blood donation management
  2. To ensure a reliable communication between donors and patients whilst maintaining privacy.
  3. To make it easier to communicate with specialized doctors.
  4. To allow users contacting admins directly in emergency and in case of queries.
  5. To enable a period tracker for our female donors
  6. To allow users to track their blood.
  7. To provide a dynamic database that is storing donors' information and communicate with them easily

Who can give blood ???

You are eligible for blood donation , if you :

  • Are fit and healthy
  • Weigh over 50kg
  • Are aged between 17 and 66
  • Are over 70 and have given blood in the last two years

How often can I give blood ???

Men can give blood every 12 weeks and women can give blood every 16 weeks.
